Professional Skills for better decision making

Professional skills are crucial in the recruitment process because they play a significant role in determining whether a candidate is a good fit for a particular job and organization. These skills encompass a range of competencies and abilities that are directly related to a person's capacity to perform well in their role and contribute positively to the workplace.
